Lead Management

Your university will work more effectively if you use a lead management system in Education CRM to track and process leads in real time. The tightly integrated solution with the best CRM for education checks and maintains the quality of lead flow by managing every lead acquired from different platforms in one place.

Manage Leads

Manage leads effectively

Now effectively manage multiple leads landing on multiple platforms with lead management and multiple tools to ensure more enrollments.

Centralized leads

Centralized leads control the prospect leads obtained from many platforms at one location, removing complications like switching between different platforms.

Quality lead flow

Through the use of Dynamic Lead Flow, you can raise the quality of leads entering your system. Set a minimum verification rate and monitor as the quality of your leads changes daily. It gives you the ability to reward or penalize lead production sources based on the number of leads they can share via APIs.

Source tracking

Without storing any data, ecampus source tracking enables you to follow up on leads. It generates prospect leads while eliminating false leads. By streamlining the process, it helps in boosting enrollments.

Verification

There are always some false leads to assure potential leads Ecampus helps to auto-verify all the leads that are centralized via SMS, email, etc. It makes it easier to concentrate on prospect leads, improve enrollment processes, and scale your admissions.
